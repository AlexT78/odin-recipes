Pasta alla Bolognese

Description

The Italian classic recipe with pasta and tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1 rib celery, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/4 cup dry red wine or beef broth
  • 1/2 lb. ground beef
  • 1 jar Bertolli® Five Cheese with Asiago & Fontina Cheeses Sauce
  • 8 ounces penne pasta, cooked and drained
  1. Heat olive oil in 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium-high heat and cook onion, carrot and celery, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender.
  2. Stir in wine and cook 1 minute.
  3. Add ground beef and cook, stirring occasionally, until almost done.
  4. Stir in Sauce. Bring to a boil over high heat.
  5. Reduce heat to low and simmer uncovered, stirring occasionally, 5 minutes or until ground beef is done.
  6. Serve over hot penne.
